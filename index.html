About Microsoft

Our Mission

Microsoft's mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. We believe technology can and should be a force for good and that meaningful innovation contributes to a brighter world in the future and today.

Our Story

Founded in 1975, Microsoft is a global leader in software, services, devices, and solutions that help people and businesses realize their full potential. Our headquarters is located in Redmond, Washington, and we operate in more than 190 countries worldwide.
